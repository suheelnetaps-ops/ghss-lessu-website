Our History

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1960

Government Higher Secondary School Lessu has been serving the educational needs of the people of Lessu Brannard, Zone Vailoo, Tehsil Larnoo, District Anantnag since 1960.

Over the decades, the institution has earned the trust of the community through quality education, dedicated teachers, and a strong commitment to academic, cultural, and moral development.

Today, the school continues to inspire students by providing an environment where learning, discipline, creativity, and leadership flourish together.
